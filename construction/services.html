Building & Construction

ABCDgreat contractors Nig. Ltd. manage and coordinate all project engineering, procurement, module fabrication, and construction services. This project delivery method offers Clients peace of mind and affords us the complete control on all project phases resulting in significant savings on time and cost and ease logistics burdens on the Clients.

Preconstruction Planning

ABCDgreat contractors Nig. Ltd. recognize the importance of effective and efficient pre-construction planning to project success, which is the reason we come on board early in the project life cycle. We work with the client and other stakeholders to define projects, develop scope, cost and schedule to execute capital projects with the most efficient use of resources and fund.

Construction Management

Our team controls all the details involved in Pre-construction, Construction and Post-Construction activities to ensure superior results. We apply our strategic expertise in critical areas of planning, design, budgeting, cost control and scheduling to ensure projects are executed within fiscal guidelines.
